Usage Notes

  • SVG format: All logos are vector SVGs - they scale to any size without quality loss.
  • Converting to PNG: Open any SVG in a browser, right-click, "Save as image" or use an SVG-to-PNG converter.
  • Favicon: Use icon-square.svg, convert to .ico format at 16x16, 32x32, 48x48 sizes.
  • Colors: Pure black (#000000) and white (#ffffff) only - maximum contrast, maximum simplicity.
  • Font: Helvetica Neue / Arial - clean, neutral, widely available.
