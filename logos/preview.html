JTF News Logo Concepts

Based on the whitepaper: "If we stay boring enough, we might just change the world."

Concept 1: The Filter

Facts being distilled down - represents stripping away adjectives and keeping only verified facts. The funnel narrows from noise to truth.

Concept 2: Two Sources

Two lines converging to a single point of truth - represents the core methodology of requiring two unrelated sources for verification.

Concept 3: The Checkpoint

A minimal verification mark - represents fact-checking and verification. Uses monospace font for a data/technical feel.

Concept 4: Clean Typography

Ultra-minimal wordmark - embodies "boring enough to change the world." No symbol, just stark typography. Maximum restraint.

Concept 5: The Balance

Abstracted scales representing objectivity and fairness. Classic symbol of justice/balance, reflecting "Fair. Balanced. Factual."
