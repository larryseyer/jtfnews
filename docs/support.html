Facts should not depend on who funds them.

Why This Matters

Most news today is shaped by the need to capture attention. Headlines are written to provoke, not to inform. Adjectives replace data. Speculation replaces verification. And behind it all, ownership structures remain invisible to the audience.

The result: people who want to know what actually happened have nowhere reliable to turn. Every source carries editorial weight. Every headline carries opinion. The facts are there, but they are buried.

JTF News exists to change that.

The JTF Methodology

Two sources. Different owners. Strip the adjectives. State the facts. Stop.

Every fact we broadcast is confirmed by at least two independent sources with different owners and different investors. AI extracts only verifiable claims and removes all opinion, speculation, and descriptive language. What remains is the record.

No ads. No tracking. No paywalls. Just the record.

We do not sell attention. We do not profile our audience. We do not place content behind paywalls. Every story, every correction, every line of code is public. Source ownership is disclosed alongside every story so you can see who funds the information you receive.

Our methodology is fully documented and version-controlled. Anyone can read it. Anyone can adopt it. No one can change it without public record.

The methodology belongs to no one. It serves everyone.
