Contributors

By the Numbers

Contributors are scored by the same four metrics as institutional sources. Numbers only. No labels.

Each contributor is evaluated on four metrics, weighted by importance:

  • Accuracy (weight: 4) — How often submitted facts are verified by independent sources.
  • Bias (weight: 3) — Editorial neutrality of submissions. Higher means less detected slant.
  • Speed (weight: 2) — How quickly facts are submitted relative to the event.
  • Consensus (weight: 1) — How often submissions align with the majority of verified sources.

Composite score = (accuracy × 4 + bias × 3 + speed × 2 + consensus × 1) ÷ 10
