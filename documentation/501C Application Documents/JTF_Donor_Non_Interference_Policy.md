# Just the Facts News

## Donor Non-Interference Policy

*Last Updated: 2026-02-14*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Principle

Financial contributions shall not influence editorial methodology,
verification standards, inclusion thresholds, or publication timing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Restrictions

Donors may not:

-   Request coverage of specific individuals or events\
-   Influence source selection\
-   Influence correction decisions\
-   Influence publication order

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Transparency

Major donors shall be disclosed in annual transparency reports in
accordance with applicable law.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Board Oversight

The Board shall review any potential donor influence concerns and
document findings.
