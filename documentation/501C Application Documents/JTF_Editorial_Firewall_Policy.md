# Just the Facts News

## Editorial Firewall Policy

*Last Updated: 2026-02-14*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Separation of Functions

Operational, fundraising, and editorial methodology functions shall
remain structurally separate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Methodology Authority

Editorial methodology is governed by documented rules and version
control. Changes require:

-   Written proposal\
-   Public documentation\
-   Board acknowledgment

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. No External Direction

No donor, sponsor, board member, or staff member may override
verification thresholds or publication rules.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Audit Mechanism

Periodic internal audits shall verify adherence to documented
methodology.
