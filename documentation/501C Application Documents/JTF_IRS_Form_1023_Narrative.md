# Just the Facts News

## IRS Form 1023 -- Narrative Description of Activities

*Last Updated: 2026-02-14*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Organizational Purpose

Just the Facts News (JTF News) is organized exclusively for charitable
and educational purposes under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue
Code.

The organization develops and distributes a structured, transparent
methodology for factual reporting without commentary, opinion, or
advocacy. Its purpose is to provide the public with accessible summaries
of verifiable recent events using defined verification and sourcing
standards.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Primary Activities

### A. Structured Factual Reporting

JTF News operates a continuously updated global news stream. Each
published item:

-   Meets predefined inclusion thresholds for public significance\
-   Is corroborated by at least two unrelated qualifying sources\
-   Is processed through rule-based language transformation to remove
    evaluative or speculative language\
-   Includes source ownership transparency where publicly available

No opinion, commentary, endorsements, or calls to action are published.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### B. Methodology Publication

The organization publishes:

-   Public documentation of verification standards\
-   Ownership disclosure methodology\
-   Correction logs\
-   Version-controlled updates

All methodology materials are publicly accessible for educational
purposes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### C. Community Replication

JTF News provides open licensing (CC-BY-SA) for communities to adopt the
methodology for local informational channels, including civic reporting,
school board summaries, and sports results.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Political Activity Compliance

JTF News does not:

-   Participate in or intervene in political campaigns\
-   Endorse or oppose candidates\
-   Rate political actors\
-   Engage in lobbying or advocacy

Government actions may be reported as structured factual events without
evaluative language.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Revenue Sources

Revenue is derived from:

-   Individual donations\
-   Foundation grants\
-   Optional early-access technical memberships

No advertising revenue is accepted. No donor may influence editorial
methodology or content selection.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Public Benefit

The organization provides an educational public benefit by:

-   Increasing transparency in sourcing\
-   Providing structured factual summaries\
-   Maintaining open documentation\
-   Supporting informed civic awareness without persuasion

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Governance & Oversight

JTF News is governed by an independent Board of Directors.
Conflict-of-interest disclosures are required annually. Editorial
methodology changes require documented board review.
