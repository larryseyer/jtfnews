# Just the Facts News

## Conflict of Interest Policy

*Last Updated: 2026-02-14*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Purpose

This policy protects the integrity of JTF News and ensures compliance
with Section 501(c)(3).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Definition of Conflict

A conflict of interest exists when a director, officer, or key employee
has a financial, ownership, or governance interest in:

-   A media outlet covered by JTF News\
-   A technology vendor used by JTF News\
-   Any entity that could benefit from organizational decisions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Disclosure Requirement

All directors and officers must:

-   Submit annual written disclosures\
-   Update disclosures upon material change\
-   Recuse themselves from related votes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Board Review

The Board shall determine whether a conflict exists and document:

-   The nature of the conflict\
-   Recusal actions\
-   Final decision

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Enforcement

Violations may result in removal from position by majority board vote.
