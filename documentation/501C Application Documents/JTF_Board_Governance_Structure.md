## Board Governance Structure

*Last Updated: 2026-02-14*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Governance Structure Overview

    Board of Directors
    │
    ├── Executive Director
    │   ├── Operations
    │   ├── Technology & Methodology
    │   └── Compliance & Transparency
    │
    ├── Audit & Compliance Committee
    │
    ├── Governance & Ethics Committee
    │
    └── Development (Fundraising) Committee

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Roles

### Board of Directors

-   Strategic oversight
-   Conflict-of-interest enforcement
-   Approval of major policy changes

### Executive Director

-   Daily operational management
-   Implementation of board-approved policies

### Audit & Compliance Committee

-   IRS compliance oversight
-   Financial transparency
-   Methodology adherence review

### Governance & Ethics Committee

-   Conflict-of-interest monitoring
-   Policy review
-   Board evaluation

### Development Committee

-   Fundraising strategy
-   Donor compliance monitoring
