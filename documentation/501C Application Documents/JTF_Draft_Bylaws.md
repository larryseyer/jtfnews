# Just the Facts News

## Draft Bylaws (Foundational Language)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article I -- Purpose

The Corporation is organized exclusively for charitable and educational
purposes within the meaning of Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue
Code.

The purpose of the Corporation is to develop and distribute structured
factual reporting methodologies and publicly accessible summaries of
verifiable events without commentary or political advocacy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article II -- Political Activity Restrictions

The Corporation shall not: - Participate in or intervene in any
political campaign on behalf of or in opposition to any candidate for
public office. - Engage in substantial lobbying activities. - Publish
endorsements or ratings of political actors.

The Corporation may publish structured factual reporting concerning
government actions provided such reporting contains no advocacy,
evaluative framing, or calls to action.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article III -- Governance

The Corporation shall be governed by an independent Board of Directors.
Directors must disclose conflicts of interest annually.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article IV -- Transparency

The Corporation shall maintain: - Public documentation of methodology -
A corrections log - Financial transparency reports - Version-controlled
methodological changes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Article V -- Dissolution

Upon dissolution, assets shall be distributed for one or more exempt
purposes within the meaning of Section 501(c)(3).
